Key findings
🏆

Highest paying roles

AI Researcher ($46,128), ML Engineer ($41,360), and Product Manager ($40,940) lead in average salary — all above the dataset mean of $34,554.

⚠️

Most at-risk roles

Data Analyst ($20,310 avg) and Business Analyst ($24,783) are the lowest-paid titles — yet Business Analyst has the most job openings (33.6M).

🌍

USA leads on salary

USA averages $47,629 — 3.5× higher than India ($13,615). UK ($38,176) and Australia ($36,915) follow. All 6 countries share similar avg risk scores (~0.395).

💡

Most in-demand skill

Python dominates with 2,430 records. Excel has the highest AI risk score (0.75) while Deep Learning commands the highest avg salary at $46,128.

📈

Risk is rising over time

Average AI risk score grew from 0.295 in 2015 to an estimated 0.491 by 2035 — a 66% increase — as automation becomes more pervasive across roles.

👤

Experience drives salary

Senior workers earn $48,062 on average vs $22,633 for Entry-level — a 2.1× gap. Risk scores are nearly identical across all experience levels (~0.396).
