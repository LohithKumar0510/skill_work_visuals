Key findings
🤖

Automation risk by sector

Manufacturing (72%) and retail (58%) face the highest automation risk, while healthcare (18%) and education (15%) remain largely resilient.

📈

AI skill premium growing

Workers with AI/ML proficiency earn 35–45% more than peers in the same role. The premium has doubled since 2019.

🌍

Regional divide widening

North America and East Asia lead in AI adoption. Sub-Saharan Africa and South Asia show slower transitions but high growth potential.

🎓

Reskilling urgency

54% of workers will need significant reskilling by 2025. Companies investing in upskilling see 2.4× higher retention rates.

⚖️

Gender gap in AI roles

Women hold only 26% of AI and data science roles globally, though the gap is narrowing at 1.2 percentage points per year.

🚀

Top future-proof skills

Critical thinking, creativity, and complex problem-solving rank as the least automatable skills through 2035.
